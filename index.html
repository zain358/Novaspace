EARTH

Earth is the only known planet to harbor life, home to billions of species and incredible biodiversity. Its stable orbit and ideal distance from the Sun make it the perfect place for life to thrive.

Like other planets, Earth orbits the Sun as part of the Solar System. What sets it apart is its oxygen-rich atmosphere and abundant water, creating a unique environment unlike any other.

Atmosphere

Rich in oxygen, nitrogen, and other life-sustaining elements.

Climate

Temperatures range from -89°C to 58°C, with varied climates from tropical to polar.

Physical

70% water, 30% land—earning the title "Blue Planet."
